All about Persian Cats

They are medium sized cats with flat faces, small ears, and very long fur. They do have some health issues like kidney disease. Their lifespan is about 12-17 years. There are some varients of them like the Dollface Persian, Himilayan Persian, and the Traditional Persian. Fun fact: An Exotic Shorthair cat is a mix of a Persian cat and a Shorthair cat.
